Guidelines for Fresh / Renewal Registration
of
Chawki Rearers / Seed Producers / Seed Cocoon Producers

Fresh Registrations of Chawki Rearers/Seed Producers

  1. Upload SSLC Marks Card
  2. Upload 3 Months Training Certificate
  3. Provide Information on Manpower

Renewal Registrations of Chawki Rearers/Seed Producers

  1. All the applicants should upload copy of the previous registration certificate.
  2. Regd. Seed Producers Should Undergo 1 Week Refresher Training at Recognised SSPC's of Central Silk Board.
  3. Regd. Chawki Rearers Should Undergo 1 Week Refresher Training at KSSRDI,Thalaghattapura and APSSRDI,Hindupur

Fresh/Renewal Registrations of Seed Cocoon Producers

  1. Fresh and Renewal Seed Cocoon Producer's Applications Should Route Through Departments Only, Direct Applications are not Entertained.
  2. Renewal Applicants Should Upload Previous Registration Certificate Copy.
